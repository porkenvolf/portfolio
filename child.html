Captain Bolorto
Captain Bolorto was my first prototype game. It was made in the pico-8 game engine and it took me about a month to complete including all the assets, and the music.

I drew heavy inspiration from the game DoDonPachi since I wanted to focus on learning the tool instead of the design of the game itself.
Also I took it as a personal challenge to make the best shmup I possibly could, so I tried to emulate the best out there.

It features one level, one boss fight, a scoring system that incentivizes aggressive playing, and multiplayer coop.
